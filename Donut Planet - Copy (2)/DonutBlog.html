#9/9/2023
○Update 1.4:

Fully reworked the profile system and kind of removed the old profile. So now there is a homelounge which you will be taken to instead of a profile.

The home lounge is basically gonna be your own personal relaxing place within Donut Planet. It will be specifically tailored to you with many customization options and upcoming features.

The old profiles are here to stay but they wont be focused on, you still have them and your designs are still saved so don't worry. You can visit your old profiles in the home area.

Welcome the new profiles, where by spending 5000 sprinkles you can buy your own customizable profile that can be viewed by the entire Donut Planet Community. It will be yours! Your own creation. You can customize your bio, your poster, your social links, your banner, your profile name and your profile picture specifically to what you desire! (As of right now I am yet to add the buying option, but its coming real soon.) Will also be adding badges to special profiles.

Now, I will present my roadmap for the end of this year. Just keep in mind that I may not even work on any of this or even commit to any of it.
